The Best Movies According to Adrian

My top 3 movies off all time.

Life of Pi

This is about a young boy who is stranded in the ocean along with a dangerous tiger.

3 Idiots

This is a story about how engineering students survive and struggles in a university. It shows courage, sorrows, and resolution on what each individual wanted in their life.

Rewind

This movie is about why things happen. How God wanted us to feel things for the benefit of all. Make your life meaningful and love your family.
